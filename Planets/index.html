Planets and Their Features

Sun

The Sun is the star at the center of the Solar System.

Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and has a very hot surface.

Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is known for its thick atmosphere.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and is the only known planet to support life.

Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is often called the "Red Planet".

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System and has a strong magnetic field.

Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and is known for its prominent ring system.

Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and is tilted on its side.

Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun and is known for its blue color.
